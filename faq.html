Do eco-friendly cleaners work as well as normal cleaning chemicals?
Eco products clean work well but for homes with a high dirt level you may just need bleach. If this is the case we might not be the best match for you. In order to determine this, we recommend texting us pictures of the problem areas to 509-903-8025.
What if something is damaged during the cleaning?
We recommend that you either put away valuable/breakable items or make a note to not have them touched. If an accidental breakage does occur we will contact you immediately to arrange a replacement/repair.
Is your company pet-friendly?
We love pets, however, if you feel you have an aggressive pet or a pet that will interfere with our work, we suggest you isolate them from the cleaners, or kindly book your appointment when someone is there to help. If you have an indoor-only cat please let us know so we can make sure your pet does not get out.
