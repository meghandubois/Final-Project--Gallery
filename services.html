Types of Cleaning

Standard Cleaning

  • All surfaces are sanitized/cleaned (counters, sinks, outside of cabinets)
  • Stove Top
  • Microwave
  • Toaster
  • Outside of Appliances
  • General Dusting
  • Toilets
  • Tubs/Showers
  • Sinks
  • Vacuuming/Mopping of all floors
  • Trash is taken out

Deep Cleaning

  • All Standard Clean Items
  • Inside of Cabinets and Drawers (only if the home is empty)
  • Inside Fridge/Freezer
  • Inside Oven
  • Baseboards
  • Blinds
  • Window Sills/Tracks
  • Ceiling Tans (if any)
  • Patio Swept (if weather permits)
  • Sliding Glass Doors are Spot Cleaned
  • Light Switch Covers
  • Light Fixtures
  • Doors

Vacation Rental

  • All Standard Clean Items
  • Deep Clean Items are Cycled Through as Needed
  • Supplies are restocked (if access is given)
  • Sheets/Towels are Washed and Restocked (only if washer/dryer is on the property)

What We Don't Do

  • Lift and move heavy furniture if it requires another persons assistance
  • Reach heights inaccessible on a 2-step ladder
  • Professional window, carpet, or grout cleaning (but we can refer you to some)
  • Clean bio-hazardous substances (such as blood, mold, or animal waste)
